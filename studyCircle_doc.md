
### StudyCircle: Product roadmap
StudyCircle is a social learning layer for Shikho that helps new students move from signup to first learning session by connecting them with relevant peers through an AI-guided onboarding flow. The product’s goal is to increase activation and retention by making learning feel supported, familiar, and socially accountable. [blog.coursera](https://blog.coursera.org/finding-a-sense-of-community-in-your-online-learning-journey/)

### Problem
A meaningful share of registered students do not reach the first real content session, which means the product loses users before they experience the core value. StudyCircle addresses this by adding a social and personalized layer at the point where drop-off happens. [loyalty](https://loyalty.cx/edtech-retention-problem/)

### Product hypothesis
If a new student is matched into a small, relevant study circle during onboarding, then they will be more likely to start the first lesson, come back, and stay engaged because the product gives them belonging and accountability, not just content. Community and study-group patterns in edtech repeatedly show that social structure can improve completion and retention. [linkedin](https://www.linkedin.com/pulse/coursera-should-consider-open-access-certifications-increase-romeo)

## V1

### V1 goal
Make onboarding lead to a real social connection and a first shared study action. The goal is to validate whether matched peer groups improve activation. [ideaplan](https://www.ideaplan.io/blog/product-roadmap-template-for-edtech)

### V1 features
- AI onboarding assistant asks about class, goal, weak subjects, study time, and language preference.
- AI creates a short student profile and recommends 3 matching study circles.
- Student joins one circle and sees a circle home with members, shared goal, and next study session.
- A simple activity feed supports only a few actions: “joined,” “studied,” “stuck,” “join me.”
- Leave/rejoin state persists so the experience feels real and durable.

### V1 non-goals
- No public forum.
- No full chat product.
- No leaderboards.
- No creator tools.
- No advanced moderation automation.

### V1 success metrics
- Signup-to-first-circle-join rate.
- Signup-to-first-lesson-play rate.
- 7-day return rate.
- Circle engagement rate within the first week.


## V2

### V2 goal
Strengthen the community loop so students come back because the group is actively helping them learn. V2 should prove that social accountability improves retention beyond onboarding. [zigpoll](https://www.zigpoll.com/content/communityled-growth-tactics-strategy-guide-director-customer-retention-focus-c3a2a7)

### V2 features
- Weekly study goals and progress check-ins.
- Peer nudges like “your circle is studying now.”
- Lightweight reactions and comments.
- Session scheduling for group study.
- Tutor or mentor drop-ins for stuck circles.
- Better matching rules using performance, pace, and attendance signals.

### V2 success metrics
- Weekly active users in circles.
- Average interactions per student per week.
- Session attendance rate.
- Course/video completion lift among circle members.

V2 is where the product starts behaving more like a retention engine than a matching feature. [greta](https://greta.agency/blog/duolingo-retention-engine)

## V3

### V3 goal
Turn StudyCircle into a scalable learning community system with personalization, growth loops, and stronger control. This version should support larger-scale adoption without losing relevance. [ideaplan](https://www.ideaplan.io/blog/product-roadmap-for-edtech)

### V3 features
- Smarter AI matching that learns from behavior over time.
- Multiple circle types, such as exam prep, weak-subject support, and same-time study rooms.
- Leaderboards or cohort challenges.
- Community moderation and trust controls.
- Referral loops and invite-based growth.
- Analytics for activation, retention, and learning outcomes.

### V3 success metrics
- Retention lift versus non-circle users.
- Referrals per active student.
- Match-to-active-circle conversion.
- Reduction in early dropout.


- V1 validates whether AI-guided study-circle matching improves activation.
- V2 strengthens habit formation through accountability and social activity.
- V3 scales the system into a deeper community layer with smarter personalization and growth.

## One-line thesis
StudyCircle is not a social feature for its own sake; it is a personalized learning connection layer that helps students reach the first lesson, stay engaged, and return more often.
